LM317T 5V to 3.3V Regulator Circuit

Component List

  • U1: LM317T adjustable voltage regulator
  • R1: 30Ω (three 10Ω resistors in series)
  • R2: 50Ω (two 20Ω resistors and one 10Ω in series)
  • C1: 0.1μF ceramic capacitor (input filtering)
  • C2: 10μF electrolytic capacitor (input stability)
  • C3: 100μF electrolytic capacitor (output stability)
  • LED: Optional power indicator
  • R3: 470Ω current limiting resistor for LED

Technical Notes

  • Output voltage: 3.33V (Vout = 1.25V × (1 + 50Ω/30Ω))
  • Safe for powering Raspberry Pi Zero W
  • Input voltage: ~5V (requires at least 1.5V above output voltage)
  • Keep all components close together for best performance
  • Ensure good grounding
